About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Melbourne, advised by Prof. Naveed Akhtar. My thesis focuses on benchmarking video understanding. My broader research interests include the generalization and robustness of neural networks, responsible data augmentation with diffusion models, vision-language models, and consistent character generation.

Alongside my PhD, I work as a Senior Scientist – Autonomy AI at Neubility. Previously, I was Chief Technology Officer (CTO) – Gen AI at Upendi.app, a Research Scientist – CV/ML at CoreMax Technology, and a Research Associate at Sejong University, all in Seoul, South Korea.

I received my M.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from Sejong University, Seoul, where I worked on image understanding and reconstruction, including an image compression method presented at CVPRw. I have attended several summer schools and served as a technical volunteer at top AI/ML conferences.
